Second, if the definition token sequence for either kind of macro contain a '##' operator then just after replacement of the parameters, each '##' is deleted together with any white space on either side, so as to concatenate the adjacent tokens and form a new token. The effect is undefined if invalid tokens are producted or if the result depends on the order of processing of the '##' operators. Also '##' may not appear at the beginning or end of a replacement toekn sequence.
