struct tnode {
  char tword[20];
  int count;
  struct tnode *left;
  struct tnode *right;
};

struct tnode s, *sp;



1. 's' is a structure and 'sp' is a pointer to a structure.
2. 'sp->count' refers to the count field of the structure point 'sp'.
3. 's.left' refers to the left subtree pointer of the structure 's'.
4. 's.right->tword[0]' refers to the first character of the 'tword' member of the 'right' subtree of the structure 's'.
