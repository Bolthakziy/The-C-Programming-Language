'x3d[i][j][k]' is equivalent to '*(x3d[i][j] + k)'. The first subexpression 'x3d[i][j]' is converted to type 'pointer to array of integers', the addition involvesmultiplication by the size of an integer. It follows from the rules that arrays are stored by rows and that the first subscript in the declaration helps determine the amount of storage consumed by an array but plays no other part in subscript calculations.
