In an old-style function declaration 'T D',where 'D' has the form,

D1(identifier-list(opt))

and the type of identifier in the declaration 'T', 'D1' is "type-modifier T", the type of the identifier of 'D' is "type-modifier" function of unspecified arguments returning 'T'.

the parametes have the form,

identifier-list:
  identifier
  identifier-list, identifier
